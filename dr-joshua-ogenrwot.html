Dr. Joshua Ogenrwot

A Visionary leader

Dr. Joshua Ogenrwot, hailing from Gulu, Uganda, is a distinguished figure in the field of veterinary medicine. Graduating with a degree in Veterinary Medicine from Makerere University, he has emerged as a beacon of expertise and compassion in his community. Fondly referred to as "a friend of animals," Dr. Ogenrwot's commitment to the well-being of creatures great and small is widely acclaimed.

His intelligence and proficiency in veterinary care have earned him the reputation as one of the foremost veterinarians in Uganda. Dr. Ogenrwot's dedication to continuous learning and innovation ensures that he remains at the forefront of veterinary science, employing the latest techniques and treatments to benefit his patients. His empathetic approach and ability to connect with both animals and their owners have endeared him to the community.

Today, Dr. Joshua Ogenrwot is the proud founder and CEO of VoskozVet Services, a veterinary clinic based in Gulu, Uganda. Through VoskozVet, he continues to provide top-notch veterinary care, offering a wide range of services to meet the diverse needs of his clients. Dr. Ogenrwot's unwavering commitment to the welfare of animals and his outstanding professionalism have solidified his position as a trusted figure in both the veterinary profession and the local community.

Dedicated to animal health with 24/7 availability, providing top-tier treatment, care, and support for all creatures, ensuring their well-being.

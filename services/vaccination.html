Vaccines for Every Creature!

Welcome to our vaccination assistance page! Whether you need help protecting your beloved pets or valuable livestock from diseases, we're here for you. Explore our resources and services to ensure your animals stay healthy and happy.

New Castle

Highly contagious viral infection in birds, causing respiratory and nervous system problems. Vaccination prevents outbreaks, safeguarding poultry populations..

Rabies

Deadly virus spread through bites, causing paralysis and death. Vaccination prevents rabies transmission, protecting pets and humans..

Fowl Pox

Viral disease causing skin lesions and respiratory issues in chickens. Vaccination reduces transmission and protects flock health.

Gumboro Disease (Infectious Bursal Disease)

Viral illness affecting young chickens, causing immune suppression and increased susceptibility to infections. Vaccination prevents Gumboro outbreaks in poultry.

Salmonella

Bacterial infection affecting birds and humans, leading to diarrhea and fever. Vaccination reduces Salmonella transmission in poultry, minimizing foodborne illnesses.

Infectious Bronchitis

Viral respiratory disease in poultry, leading to respiratory distress and decreased egg production. Vaccination helps control outbreaks and maintains flock productivity.

Foot and Mouth disease

Highly contagious viral infection affecting cloven-hoofed animals, causing fever and blisters in the mouth and feet. Vaccination limits the spread of Foot & Mouth Disease and protects livestock industries.

Anthrax

Bacterial disease affecting livestock, wildlife, and humans, causing severe illness and death. Vaccination prevents Anthrax outbreaks and minimizes public health risks..

Black Quarter (Black leg)

Bacterial infection in cattle, causing severe muscle swelling and death. Vaccination provides immunity against Black Quarter, reducing economic losses in livestock farming.
